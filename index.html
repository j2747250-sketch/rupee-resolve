Common Issues

Financial problems
that deserve real answers.

If any of these sound familiar, you are not alone — and these issues can be resolved with the right guidance.

CIBIL / Credit Report Errors

Wrong entries, outdated records, or accounts you never opened are silently damaging your credit score.

Hidden Bank Charges

Unexplained deductions, maintenance fees, or charges you never agreed to — affecting your account without notice.

Name / Record Misspellings

A misspelled name or wrong date in a financial record can block loans, insurance, and government benefits.

Fake Loans / Insurance

Loans or insurance policies opened in your name without your knowledge, often a result of identity misuse.

EMI Disputes

Incorrect EMI calculations, payments not reflected, or lender records that don't match your payment history.

Payment Aggregator Issues

Failed transactions held as settled, duplicate charges, or refund delays from payment gateways and UPI platforms.

What We Handle

Our services,
clearly stated.

We focus on the financial issues that institutions often ignore — and help you navigate them with precision.

Credit

CIBIL & Credit Report Disputes

We help you identify and dispute inaccurate entries in your credit report — from wrongly reported defaults to accounts that were never yours.

Banking

Bank Charge Disputes

Unclear deductions, unauthorized fee structures, or charges applied without consent — we help you challenge them through the right channels.

Records

Record Correction Support

Wrong names, dates of birth, or address mismatches in banking, insurance, or loan records — we guide you through the correction process.

Fraud

Fake Loan & Insurance Entries

Fraudulent entries created in your name without consent. We help you raise formal disputes and get them removed from your financial profile.

Loans

EMI & Loan Disputes

Payments not reflected, incorrect outstanding balances, or pre-closure penalties applied unfairly — we help you build a clear case.

Payments

Payment Aggregator Issues

Failed UPI payments marked successful, refund delays, or duplicate charges — we help you escalate these to the right authority.
